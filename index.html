INGRAM CARIBBEAN SUMMIT 2015

Agenda

Schedule

June 23, 2015

11:00 – 18:00 - Arrival

Welcome to the city where big decisions are made! Our travel agent is on stand-by to help you with your travels. Please make sure to reach out to our staff if you have any special requests at the following email: ingramcaribbeansummit2015@verlasinc.com

11:00 – 18:00 - Your VIP Registration

We are rolling out the blue carpet to greet you in style at the trendy Melrose Hotel in Georgetown. Your room awaits…

20:00 – 22:00 - At the River Welcome Cocktail

HP greets you like the head of state! Take in the greatest views from the side of Potomac river and enjoy a night of cocktails, networking and scrumptious food.

June 24, 2015

07:00 – 08:00 - Rise and Shine

Set your alarms, because this day is packed with adventure! Grab your breakfast and meet us and our vendors.

08:00 – 09:00 - Meet the New Ingram Micro

Ingram Micro welcomes you to our 2015 Caribbean Summit. Learn about our vision for the coming year, and the improvements we are making to take your business to the next level. Think E-Commerce, Training, Loyalty & more!

09:00 – 10:00 - Keynote Session: Where Big Decisions Are Made

Speaker: Dennis Crupi - Director, Creative Services Ingram Micro.
Our Keynote speaker will discuss the impact of powerful decisions and how they can shift paradigms and transform businesses.

10:15 – TBA - The Great Eaton Escape

Old Teambuilding Activities belong in a museum. This year, come prepared for the adrenaline rush you’d never expect to get in DC with Eaton! Only 30% of participants manage to “escape” this amazing, nail-biting activity – will you be one of them?

10:15 – TBA - In 1-on-1 We Trust

Make monumental decisions with your key vendors in individually scheduled sessions. Interested in specific vendors? Click on our SPONSOR site to choose your must-see sponsors for a private meeting.

12:45 – 13:45 - HP Food for Thought: Mauricio de Hoyos

HP will highlight the latest transformations that will make your solutions decisions easier. Think you’ve seen it all? Think again!

20:30 – 23:30 - The White (and Black) House Party

Bring out the black and red attire for this private farewell dinner & entertainment as we celebrate our time together in Washington D.C. This marks the beginning of a new era, and we are glad that you have decided to join us.

June 25, 2015

07:30 – 08:30 - Breakfast for Champions

June is perfect for a day on the town – and today is no exception! Set your alarms – this is one trip you do not want to miss!

08:30 – 12:00 - The APC Trivia Tour

If you think Washington DC is all monuments and museums…think again! Our APC Trivia tour is specially designed for you to enjoy the outing – without making it a history lesson. Snap your picture, hashtag it and enjoy the landmarks.

14:00 – 18:00 - Digging for Gold – Nuggets of Great Business Decisions

Our Gold Sponsors are ready to showcase their experience in making decisions that have changed the industry – let’s tune in to their knowledge, and see what can be applied in our own businesses.

Presenting Sponsors: Beats by Apple, Citrix, Kingston, Lenovo

08:00 – 11:30 - Anchors, away!

Welcome aboard…indulge in the experience of a private yacht for an evening of style & networking. Take in the beautiful skyline as we cruise through the Potomac, and watch the magic unfold.

June 26, 2015

07:00 – 12:00 - Departures

Is it really over? Parting is such sweet sorrow! We enjoyed hosting you in Washington DC and hope to see you in your markets soon! Please let us know how we did by filling out the surveys emailed to you post-event. We look forward to next year’s event.
